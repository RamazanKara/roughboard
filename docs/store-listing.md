## Full description (≤4000 chars)

```
Roughboard is a fast, fully offline whiteboard for hand-drawn sketches, diagrams, flowcharts, and quick notes. Everything you draw stays on your device.

Built on the open-source Excalidraw editor, Roughboard brings its loved sketchy, hand-drawn look to Android as a proper offline app — no account, no sign-in, no internet required.

FEATURES
• Hand-drawn style shapes — rectangles, ellipses, diamonds, arrows and lines
• Freehand drawing and an eraser
• Text and image insertion
• A reusable shape library
• Full styling — stroke and fill colours, stroke width, opacity, layering, fonts
• Select, move, resize and group elements
• Infinite canvas with smooth zoom and pan
• Light and dark mode
• Export and share as PNG, SVG, or an editable .excalidraw file via the Android share sheet
• Open existing .excalidraw files

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• 100% offline — the app makes no network calls
• No account, no tracking, no ads, no analytics
• Your drawings never leave your device

Great for brainstorming, wireframing, diagramming, teaching, and visual note-taking — on phones and tablets.

Roughboard is an independent app and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Excalidraw. It is built on the open-source Excalidraw editor (MIT licensed).
```
