What it is

Roughboard packages the open-source Excalidraw editor into a native, fully offline Android app — sketches, diagrams, flowcharts and notes with a natural hand-drawn style. No account, no sign-in, no internet required; your drawings never leave your device.

Features

  • Hand-drawn shapes, arrows, freehand, text & images
  • Reusable shape library, full styling, layers
  • Infinite canvas, zoom & pan, light/dark mode
  • Export & share as PNG, SVG or .excalidraw via the Android share sheet
  • 100% offline — no tracking, no ads, no analytics

Roughboard is an independent app and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Excalidraw. Built on the open-source Excalidraw editor (MIT licensed).
