# Privacy Policy — Roughboard

_Last updated: 2026-06-28_

Roughboard is designed to be fully private and offline.

## Data we collect

**None.** Roughboard does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal
data or usage data.

- All your drawings, settings, and library items are stored **locally on your
  device only** (in the app's local storage). They never leave your device and
  are not sent to us or any third party.
- The app contains **no analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting
  SDKs**.
- The app makes **no network calls** to external servers. Everything (including
  fonts) is bundled in the app and runs offline.

## Files you export or share

When you use "Save to disk" or "Export image", the file is created on your device
and handed to Android's standard share sheet **only when you explicitly trigger
it**. You choose the destination (Files, another app, etc.). Roughboard does not
upload these files anywhere.

## Permissions

Roughboard declares the `INTERNET` permission solely because the app's editor
runs inside a local in-app web layer; it is **not** used to send your data off
the device.

## Your rights

Because no data is collected or stored off-device, there is nothing for us to
access, export, or delete. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

## Children

Roughboard does not collect data from anyone, including children.
